Hot ham and Cheese

Description

A fast and comforting meal whipped up in as little a five minutes. This meal is great for active children or the rushing adult. Quick and easy

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • deli style ham
  • cheese
  • butter

Steps

  1. Heat butter in pan, enough to cover the bottom
  2. Prepare your ham sandwich cold and lightly butter both sides of bread
  3. After preparing the sandwich, add it to the pan
  4. Allow to cook in pan, flipping occasionly
  5. When both sides are toasted to satisfaction and cheese is melted, you're done!
