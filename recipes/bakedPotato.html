Baked Potato

Description

A potato served fresh out of the oven sliced down the middle for you to load with limitless toppings.

Ingredients

you reallly only need the potato to make a baked potato and olive oil I'll list some of the common toppings

  • butter
  • sour cream
  • chives
  • cheese
  • bacon bits

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 400F
  2. Poke potato with fork all over
  3. rub potato in oil and lay on cooking sheet
  4. Cook until soft in the cener
