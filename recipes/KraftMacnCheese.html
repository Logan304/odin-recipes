Kraft Mac n Cheese

Description

Macaroni and real creamy cheese come together for an unbelievably comforting meal. Enjoy a bowl of comforting Kraft Mac and Cheese in under 10 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Water
  • A box of Kraft Mac and Cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil on the stove
  2. Open your box of Mac an Cheese and remove the cheese packet
  3. Pour your noodles in to the pot and stir occasionly
  4. Let your noodles cook until they feel and look finshed
  5. Strain the noodles over the sink
  6. Add the cheese packet, 3/4th cup of milk, and 1 tbsp of butter to noodles
  7. stir until evenly mixed
